Dumplings

Description

Dumplings are essentially just meat and/or vegatables stuffed into dough. They are a simple yet delicious food.

To have dumplings for a meal, it's easiest to buy frozen dumplings and then boil them. The whole process takes about just twenty minutes to half an hour.

Ingredients

  • Frozen dumplings
  • Cooking oil
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic

Steps

Cooking the dumplings

  1. Boil a pot of water.
  2. Put the frozen dumplings into the pot of water.
  3. Cook for roughly 15 minutes, or until the dumplings rise the top of the bowl.
  4. Do a taste test to see if the skin is cooked thoroughly. If not, let the dumplings cook for a little longer.
  5. Enjoy the dumplings!

Mixing the sauce

  1. Chop the garlic into small pieces.
  2. Pour vinegar and soy sauce into a bowl.
  3. Dump the garlic into the bowl and mix.
