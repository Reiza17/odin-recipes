Scrambled Eggs

Description

Gordan Ramsay has a special recipe for scrambled eggs. His recipe involves cooking the egg slowly so that the final result is much creamier and softer than normal scrambled eggs.

I first discovered Ramsay's scrambled eggs recipe through Youtube a few years ago. Ever since, I've cooked his scrambled eggs countless times, which is my personal testament to how delicious they are.

Ingredients

  • 6 eggs
  • 15g butter
  • Salt and pepper
  • Crème fraîche

Steps

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl and mix them thoroughly.
  2. Pour the eggs into a buttered pan.
  3. Cook the eggs under low-medium heat and stir constantly.
  4. Add a table spoon of crème fraîche and stir.
  5. After the eggs are slightly cooked, lift the pan off the stove for 20 seconds.
  6. Put the pan back onto the stove and cook for another 20 seconds.
  7. Repeat Steps 5 and 6 until the eggs resemble the picture above.
  8. That's it! Enjoy your scrambled eggs.
