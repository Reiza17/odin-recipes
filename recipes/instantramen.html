Instant Ramen

Description

Instant ramen is easy to make, yet is delicious. You can buy instant ramen in any Asian supermarket, and they're usually very cheap, which makes them the perfect snack or meal for college students.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 packs of instant ramen
  • 1-2 eggs
  • Vinegar
  • Vegatables (such as brocolli)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Add the instant ramen into the pot.
  3. Cook for about 5 minutes.
  4. Crack eggs into the ramen and stir slowly.
  5. Add vegatables into the pot.
  6. Cook for another 2-3 minutes, then turn off the stove.
  7. Pour the noodles into a large bowl, and enjoy your meal!
